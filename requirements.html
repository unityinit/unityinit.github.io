Requirements

This is what your projects needs to have!

The $500 scholarship for the 2017/2018 starting school year is simple, identify either a problem or, someway you would like a community to imrpove and write a report on how you would solve that problem or, how you would improve that community. You must identify the city and state of the community of your choice. There is no word count to this report, what matters is how clearly you convey your ideas and how effective they would be if executed. Some ideas of community problems or improvement coud be: political, service projects needed, economic improvement, religious, etc. You can use the following format if you choose to:

1. Situational Analysis
2. Objectives
3. Target Audiences
4. Strategies
5. Tactics
6. Budget
7. Measurement

Once your report is complete, submit to ucinitiative@gmail.com. The winner will be chosen sometime at the beginning of September. Please use the email you would like to be contacted at. Please include your name and what school your are attending. Visit deadline to see when this report is due by.
