Unity Community Initiative

Bringing Communities Together

Unity Community Initiative is a non-profit scholarship organization that encourages and helps ambitious and creative students to make a difference in their community.

Find Out More

"Life's most persistent and urgent question is,
What are you doing for others?"
- Martin Luther King, Jr

Unity Community Initiative (UCI) started as the brainchild of college senior, Dylan Howard, who was looking for a way to empower future students and communities. Many college students across America will return to their home communities to work and build the economy. By encouraging students to start now in giving back and helping their communities our society will grow in unity. Grassroots change will strengthen the future generations to come.
